# Growth Strategy

Owner: Product. How Ai Studio Referrals grows adoption — and how the product helps its
customers grow.

## Product-led growth

- **Time-to-value in minutes.** Sign up → launch a configured campaign → generate a link →
  share → tracked conversion → reward. The onboarding path is a first-class metric (CSE owns
  it).
- **Self-service everything.** No-code campaign builder, integration registry, white-label
  settings — no engineer or sales call required.
- **The product sells itself.** Every referral link is a marketing surface; the embeddable
  widget spreads installs.

## Wedge → expand

1. **Wedge:** one high-value referral program (the customer's primary growth loop).
2. **Expand:** more campaigns, more channels (email/SMS/Slack), deeper analytics, payouts.
3. **Lock-in via value:** audit trail, fraud protection, multi-currency, integrations.

## Metrics we optimize

- Activation: % of new orgs that launch a campaign and see a first conversion.
- K-factor & viral cycle time (already computed in `stats.ts`).
- Net reward ROI for the customer (rewards paid vs. attributed value).
- Fraud caught vs. false-positive rate.

## Channels

- Marketplace listings and certified integrations (see
  [marketplace-strategy](marketplace-strategy.md)).
- Industry packs as landing-page magnets (see [industry-packs](industry-packs.md)).
- Developer-friendly public API + widget for embedded distribution.
